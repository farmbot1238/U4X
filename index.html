🤖 The beast: U4X LineTracker v2

High‑speed · Precision · Adaptive PID

Our custom-built line follower robot uses 8-channel IR sensor array, high-torque DC motors with encoder feedback, and a robust STM32 microcontroller. Every millisecond matters.

  • Adaptive PID with dynamic speed scaling
  • Carbon fiber chassis — ultra lightweight (285g)
  • Real-time telemetry & OLED dashboard
  • Li-Po battery optimized for 15min endurance

🏆 NLFRC 2026 — Line Follower Robot Competition

About the event

The NLFRC 2026 (National Line Follower Robotics Competition) is dedicated entirely to Line Following Robots — testing speed, accuracy, and stability on complex tracks with curves, intersections, and varying line widths. Our team U4X entered with a mission to showcase the pinnacle of embedded robotics.

We will compete in the final round on 2026 against top university teams. The robot must autonomously navigate a white-black track with unpredictable segments. Our preparations include AI-assisted parameter tuning and extensive track simulation.
